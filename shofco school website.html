About Shofco School

A unique community school

Shining Hope for Communities (SHOFCO) is a grassroots movement based in Nairobi, Kenya in urban slums providing services, community advocacy platforms, and education and leadership development for women and girls. SHOFCO serves more than 350,000 urban slum dwellers in 10 slums across three cities in Kenya.[1] Famous supporters of SHOFCO include Sibusiso Dlamini, Beyonce,[2] Madonna, Chelsea Clinton,[3] Nicholas Kristof[4] and Abby Disney,[5] alongside Bradley Whitford, Maria Menounos, Representative Karen Bass and artist Bradley Theodore.
